How to Submit a Listing

Above is a video tutorial which walks through the entire process of submitting a listing.

How do you submit a listing?

To start off, ensure that you are on the home page of the website, where on the left side of the page you can see all of the listing locations on a Google map, and on the right side of the page, you can see all of the listings themselves.

Navigate to the top bar and click on the "Landlord" drop-down menu.

Click the "Submit a Listing" option under the drop-down menu to enter the listing submission page.

Once you are on the listing submission page, enter in the necessary information for your listing.

Once you have entered in all of the necessary information for your listing, click the Submit button.

Your listing is now published!
