How to Submit a Review

Above is a video tutorial which walks through the entire process of submitting a listing.

How do you submit a review?

To start off, ensure that you are on the home page of the website, where on the left side of the page you can see all of the listing locations on a Google map, and on the right side of the page, you can see all of the listings themselves.

First, find the listing in which you want to submit a review for.

Click on the listing, which should take you to the listing detail page.

Scroll down toward the bottom of the page, where you can find the "Submit a review" section. Enter what you want in the fields provided.

Once you finish writing your review, click the submit button. Your review will now be published.
